<h3>Travel</h3>

<p/>
Welcome to my 2022 journey!
<p/>
I am documenting my trip around the USA here.
<p/>
My plan is to see the trees turn colors in Maine and New England this fall.
It looks like I am right on time.. after a month of traveling to get to Maine.

<p/>
Then I plan to make my way to the southeast corner of the country,
then to the southwest, and then home.
I expect to be a
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_nomad" target="_blank">digital nomad</a>
for about four months.
